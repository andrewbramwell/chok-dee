Discover Our Thai Specialties

At Chokdee, we blend fresh local ingredients with quality Thai imports. Our kitchen is stocked with fragrant lemongrass, fiery chilies, and aromatic spices straight from Thailand. These authentic elements, combined with daily fresh produce, create truly genuine Thai flavors. From tangy tom yum to rich massaman curry, each dish offers a taste of Thailand here in the beautiful town of Ilkley.

About Us

From Bangkok's Finest Kitchens to Ilkley: Our Flavorful Journey

A Taste of Thailand, Crafted with Love

We are Mod and Eak, and Chokdee is the realization of our dream. With roots in Thailand's culinary scene, we bring over two decades of experience from prestigious establishments. Our journey began at Bussaracum Thai Cuisine, one of Thailand's oldest and first fine dining Thai restaurants, where Mod started her training as a chef and where we first met. Since 2008, we've been delighting UK diners with authentic Thai flavors. In December 2022, we took the leap to open Chokdee, blending our expertise with our passion for Thai cuisine. Join us to experience the warmth and flavors of Thailand, right here in Ilkley.
